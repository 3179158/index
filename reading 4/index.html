1.  Why does Laurel Schwulst advocate for individual websites over social media? Do you agree with her? Please explain your answer.

There are many reasons Schwulst advocates personal websites over social media. Social media companies are private companies with only their needs in mind. These few companies have a monopoly on the web. The first one outlined is advertisements. Social media sites are littered with ads which not only depersonalizes the website, it also skewers the users and their perceptions. Ads also move people away from the site. A personal website gives the author control. It can present a specified theme and subject. Schwulst believes the structure of a website is as important as its content.
